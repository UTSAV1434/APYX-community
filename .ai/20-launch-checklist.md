# Launch Success Criteria

The release is successful when:

- Users can easily discover APYX.
- Event registration works flawlessly.
- The experience feels smooth and premium.
- The platform is accessible.
- Performance meets defined goals.
- Branding is consistent.
- The Design Bible has been followed.

---

# Final Principle

Never launch because a deadline has arrived.

Launch because the experience is ready.

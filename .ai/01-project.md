# APYX Project Context

## Vision

APYX is a student-led technology ecosystem focused on innovation, hackathons, workshops, seminars, networking, and real-world learning.

The website should feel like a premium technology product rather than a traditional college club website.

The goal is to create one of the highest quality community websites with immersive storytelling, premium interactions, and modern user experience.

---

## Objectives

The website should allow visitors to:

- Discover APYX
- Explore events
- Register for hackathons
- Read announcements
- Browse galleries
- Access resources
- Meet the team
- Contact APYX

---
